How it works

Your season, simplified

From your first profile to PD credits landing in your wallet, Kooee works for you every step of the way.

1

Upload your profile

Qualifications, WWCC, activity types and availability. Five minutes. Businesses can find you from day one.

2

Set your availability

Mark your calendar ahead of time, set your current status, and lock out dates you’re unavailable. Confirmed programs block automatically. Businesses see exactly when and where you’re open before they reach out.

3

Send or receive a Kooee

A business finds you and sends a Kooee, or you browse and send one to them. Accept, pass or reach out. Full briefing arrives the moment you confirm.

4

Complete the program, your field record updates

Run the program. The moment it’s done, Kooee logs it, date, location, activity, employer, duration. Add your own notes. No paperwork, no follow-up, no chasing anyone for records.

5

PD credits land in your wallet

The business contributes automatically. Credits build and redeem at approved training providers for the courses you actually want.
