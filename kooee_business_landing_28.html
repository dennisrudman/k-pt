Centralised platform

Your team's information lives across emails,
spreadsheets and memory.

Qualifications in one email, first aid certs in another, availability confirmed by text. Staff history lives in a spreadsheet that is already out of date. There is no single place to see your team clearly. Every program cycle starts from scratch.

Kooee is one place for all of it. Profiles hold qualifications, compliance and availability in a single view. Your dashboard tracks the whole team with 60-day alerts before anything expires. Instructors update their own documents. You see the status, not the inbox thread.

Staff retention and development

Experienced instructors don't burn out loudly.
They just don't come back next season.

The outdoor sector loses skilled instructors every season, not to competitors, but to industries that invest in their people. When there is no clear path forward, the decision to move on is easy. The ones who stay do so out of passion alone. That is not a retention strategy.

94%
of employees say they would stay longer at a company that invests in their learning and development.
LinkedIn Workforce Learning Report, 2022

Kooee contributes a minimum of $2 per program day per instructor as a PD credit, with the option to increase this. Register as an approved provider and run your own workshops. Instructors redeem credits with you and bring those skills back to your programs. PD contributions are a legitimate business expense.
